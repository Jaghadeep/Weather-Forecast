About Us

The purpose of this website is to provide users with up-to-date and accurate information about weather conditions and forecasts. The key features of this website include:

  • Current weather conditions: Temperature, humidity, wind speed and direction, and visibility.

  • Weather forecasts: Short-term (daily or hourly) and long-term forecasts for specific locations.

  • Radar and satellite imagery: Maps showing precipitation, clouds, and weather patterns.

  • Severe weather alerts: Notifications about severe weather conditions like storms, hurricanes, and tornadoes.

  • Customization: Ability for users to set their preferred locations and receive personalized weather updates.

  • Educational content: Information about meteorology, climate change.
